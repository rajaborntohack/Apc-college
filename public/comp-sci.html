THE DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE

The department was begun in the year 1996. Since then it has been producing excellent Computer Science graduates who are the pillars in the current and emerging information era. Excellent academicians and a sophisticated computing environment complement the learning process. The department prepares the students to face confidently the real time challenges posed in the I.T. Industry.
