Application procedure for filling up the Skill Oriented Diploma Courses

  • Student Admission Procedures (2020-2021) – for the Skill Oriented Diploma Courses sponsored by UGC are done only online (via Internet only).
  • We kindly request all the students to read the application procedure thoroughly once before applying so that you can complete the application procedures correctly.
  • Applications received with incorrect information and without sufficient information will be duly rejected. Application fee details for the course is Rs 100/.
  • Completing and submitting the application form in the website does not mean that your admission is confirmed.
  • The student will be able to apply for any course only after paying the application registration fee.
  • The application registration fee will not be refunded for any reason. It is considered the registration fee.
  • The student should ensure that she has selected the right course of her choice.
  • Student should attach a photocopy that contains the following details:

                 Bank Reference / Transaction Number

                 Application Fee

                 Bank account number

                 Bank name and branch

                 Payment day

  • Kindly save all the above details for future reference.
  • Student can pay the registration fees only through Net banking, NEFT, QR Code and Google Pay.
  • Bank details for payment of registration fee:

                 Bank Account Name A.P.C. Mahalaxmi College for Women NSQF Account

                 Bank Account No : 3858169540

                 Bank Name: Central Bank of India

                 Bank Branch: St.Mary’s Branch, Thoothukudi

                 IFSC Code: CBIN0284061

  • Receipt of application fee and application form link (link Id) will be sent to the student’s E-mail ID.
  • Application should be submitted online only. Submitting the filled in application directly or by post is not entertained.
  • The student must register on or before October 25 th 2020.
  • Subsequent application will be considered as Late Registration.
  • All enclosures must be less than 5Mb. (in JPG/JPEG format only).
  • The College Selection Committee will decide on the provisional list of selected candidates. Selected candidates will be intimated through SMS.
  • At the time of admission the Student should bring the required original documents and a copy of the admission form submitted on the website.
  • During the admission, students should bring

                 Score Certificate –Plus 1 and Plus 2 (+1, +2)

                 Acknowledgment Form (Acknowledgment Receipt given by College)

                 Community Certificate

                 Recent Passport Size Photo : 1

  • Admission is denied for the applicant who fails to bring all the above said certificates .
  • All the original certificates along with three copies of them should be submitted on the day of admission
